01 — Why Segmentum
  • The problem
  • The fix
  • The difference

Microscopy has always been the bottleneck. The analysis layer has never kept up with the imaging.

You spend weeks optimising your staining, days at the microscope collecting beautiful data, and then hours, sometimes days more, wrestling with ImageJ macros, Cellpose installations, Python environments that break, and parameter tuning that feels more like guesswork than science. Every lab reinvents the same wheel. Every student learns the same frustrating workarounds.

Segmentum fixes that. Upload any image, any format, any microscope, any cell type, and within seconds an intelligent pipeline reads your data, figures out what you're looking at, picks the right segmentation model, and hands you back clean masks, quantified measurements, and publication-ready figures. No installation. No coding. No parameter hunting.

The whole system is built by someone who does this exact analysis for their own thesis, which means it's designed around how biologists actually work, not how engineers imagine they do.
